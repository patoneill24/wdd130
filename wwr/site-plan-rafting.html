Overview

Purpose

The purpouse of this website is to provide advertising for the Dry Oar Whitewater Rafting company in order to boost revenue. The site will provide good information concerning our staff or rafting guides, dates and times of avilabity, saftey equipment avialbe to rent, the costs, and what the rapids are like. It will also provide safety information such as potential risks and dangers that come from rafting and how to have a fun and safe experience. The web site will also provide a pdf waiver form that people must fill out and sign before starting their rafting trip.

Audience

The website is targeted towards families, groups and individuals who are interested in athletics and are looking for a fun outdoor adventure. Those who are looking for this kind of adventure will want to be well informed about our saftey equipment, staff, etc. so that they can be assured that they will have a wonderful and safe experience out on the rapids. Thise website can be acessed on all electronic devices such as on mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers, but it will most likely be accesed by people on laptops or desktops.
