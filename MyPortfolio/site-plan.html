Home page

Hello, I’m Patrick O’Neill! Welcome to my portfolio website. I’ve created this site to display my resume for potential employers to look at. It provides details concerning my work experience and educational accomplishments as well as some skills and attributes I have developed over the years. This website can also provide helpful ideas for people who are trying to build their own resumes.

This site contains information concerning the many qualifications that I have as well as some things that I can contribute to a business. I’m very passionate about my professional career, and the information available in this website will illustrate that.

I’m very grateful for the time you’re taking to view my portfolio, I assure you that it is time well-spent. Here is my contact information if you like to schedule an interview with me or if you have any questions and concerns. Thank you!

Work Experience and Skills

Over the past few years I have been able to work at a variety of jobs, and at each of these jobs I was able to learn different things and acquire different skills. Here is a list of companies I have worked for, the duties that I carried out under their employ, and some skills I learned.

Little Cesar’s Pizza: March 2020-October 2020

Duties - Prepared dough and made pizzas - Serviced customers and carried out transactions as a cashier - Took pizzas in and out of oven and cut and boxed them - Prepared other foods as well such as wings and breadsticks - Clean store and kitchen - Counted store’s inventory daily

Accomplishments and Skills Acquired - Got promoted to assistant manager - Learned how to safely prepare food - Learned how to treat customers and co-workers with respect and care - Learned how to stay calm and collected during the stressful and busy store hours

Redwood Empire: June 2020-October 2020

Duties - Got hired through a contracting agency - Stacked, bundled, labeled, and packaged bunches of wood that would be used for fencing - The bunches of wood would then be shipped off to companies such as Home Depot and Lowe’s

Accomplishments and Skills Acquired - I had to complete a certain number of units by a certain time, so I had to work at a fast-paced rate while at the same time assembling the units correctly - Therefore I learned how to work fast and efficiently - Got offered a full-time job by the company because they were pleased with my performance

Missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints: October 2020-October 2022

Duties: - Served in Georgia and then later Chile - Studied the scriptures and taught people about Jesus Christ and His Gospel - Taught and instructed other missionaries - Collaborated with members of the church and other missionaries in order to help others draw closer to Jesus Christ - Set high goals and took time planning on how to achieve those goals - Studied the language of Spanish so that I could communicate with people in Chile

Accomplishments and skills acquired - Learned Spanish - Learned how to set goals and how to plan effectively - Learned how to create meaningful connections with people - Improved my ability to study and take notes - Served in leadership positions

Nike Factory Store in Gilroy, CA: October 2022-Present

Duties: - Talking and providing service to customers in English and Spanish - Moving and unpacking shipments of clothing and shoes at an efficient rate - Cashier - Persuading customers to sign up for a membership with us - Cleaning and organizing store to make it look presentable

Accomplishments and skills acquired - Learning how to communicate with consumers to better understand their needs and wants - Learning how to better collaborate with co-workers in order to get a task done - Learned a lot of organization and time management skills
